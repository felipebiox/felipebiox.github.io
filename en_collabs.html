Database Management Web Tool - OmniDB Website.

OmniDB is a web tool that simplifies database management focusing on interactivity, designed to be powerful and lightweight.

Among its funcionalities, I'm highlighting the responsive interface, unified workspace, editors and interactive tables, multiple SGBD support, graph and graphic demonstrations, customization and more.

My main contribution was its website development, OmniDB's visual identity and some icons for interface

"Melissa Contra o Tempo" - play here.

This app is an educational game targeted at children. Here, the character Melissa has to deliver icecream to her friends, before the time runs out.

To achieve the objective, the player has to take her through an urban scenery filled with obstacles, always respecting the crosswalks, in order to complete the maze and reach the destined location before time runs out.
