"Melissa Contra o Tempo" - play here.

This app is an educational game targeted at children. Here, the character Melissa has to deliver icecream to her friends, before the time runs out.

To achieve the objective, the player has to take her through an urban scenery filled with obstacles, always respecting the crosswalks, in order to complete the maze and reach the destined location before time runs out.
